Your Profile Page

Once you have an account, you can personalize your very own profile page to share the information you want with other people. Customize your name, profile picture, high school, marital status, bio, and social media links as much as you want.

Connect With Other Users

Your connections are people who attended the same high school as you. Look through the list, and send a request to someone you want to connect with. Someone may have already sent a request to you!

Looking for someone specific? Search your list of connections to easily find the person you want to reconnect with.

By toggling on Request Mode, you can also quickly send many connection requests without needing to confirm each request.

Once you've connected with someone, view their profile page and connect with them on other social media sites using the links they list in their profile.
